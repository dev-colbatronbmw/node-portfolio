MISSION

Beginning in 2007 I went on a two year volunteer mission. I did not choose the location, but was sent to the West Indies (Caribbean). I lived in Guyana in South America for one year. Guyana is on the SouthEast boarder of Venezuela. My second year was spent In Trinidad and Tobago. An Island nation just off the coast of Venezuela. I learned a lot living in these countries. The biggest lesson I took away was how to work hard. And how much you could love the people you serve.
